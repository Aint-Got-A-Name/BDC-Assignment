See Your Students
Like Never Before

Applying Big Data has been a recent trend in the world of education. It has been used to analyze and predict the performance of students. In our project, you can easily visualize the student's performance in class, using eye-catching graphs and dashboard. The project also attempts to develop ML models to predict what subjects the student should learn next to get a better result.
